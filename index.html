Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

TierMaker (“we”, “the app”) is committed to your privacy. This policy describes how we handle information when you use the TierMaker app and its iMessage extension.

Information We Do Not Collect

TierMaker does not collect, store, or transmit your personal data. We do not use analytics, advertising, or third-party tracking in the app.

Data Stored on Your Device

The app may store tier list state and preferences locally on your device (e.g., when you are in the middle of a game). This data stays on your device and is not sent to our servers.

iMessage and Apple

When you use the iMessage extension, tier list content is sent through Apple’s iMessage service. Apple’s privacy policy and terms apply to that data. We do not receive or store the contents of your messages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top will be revised when we do. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact us via the Support page.
